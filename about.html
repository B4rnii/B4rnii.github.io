About

My name is Barnabás Tiborcz Zsombor. I'm from Hungary. And I always had an urge to create something big, but I didn't know what and how I should do.

My father is a software engineer too, so when I got my first computer he immediately started teaching me C# and OOP. I slowly started getting into game development in Unity. Couple years after switching back-and-forth between Unity, Unreal and Godot, I got interested in making my own engine. I began my journey with TheCherno's game engine series. After some time I stumbled upon Ryan Fleury and Handmade Hero. I started embracing their message, low level programming and quality software, and I'm going to try my best to spread these thoughts.

These days, I dedicate much of my free time to crafting custom games, tools, softwares, and game engines, using C/C++ with minimal dependencies. You can learn more about my projects here.

This site showcases my projects and blog entries, sharing insights and experiences in software engineering and game development.

Appreciate you stopping by!
